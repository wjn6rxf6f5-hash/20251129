Black-Scholes Option Calculator

Monte Carlo estimation under risk-neutral measure. Provide your own payoff using ST (terminal price) and optional K, r, sigma, T.

Examples: max(ST - K, 0) (call), max(K - ST, 0) (put), max(abs(ST - K) - 10, 0) (strangle with band). Functions: abs, max, min, exp, log, sqrt, sin, cos, tan.
